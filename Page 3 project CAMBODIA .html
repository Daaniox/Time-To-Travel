CAMBODIA

Cambodia is located in South East Asia.

The Cambodian flag is the only one in the world to feature a building.

The country has a shocking past that happened not that long ago, however, it is a popular destination for backpackers.

Cambodians are renowned for cooking up creepy crawlies.

These include crickets, ants and even tarantulas.

Despite this, most people eat meats, fish, vegtables, and rice as part of their daily diet.

Cambodia is home to the largest freshwater lake in the whole of Southeast Asia.

The Tonle Sap provides sustenance to nearly half of cambodias crops and is a major source for fishing.

Intrestingly, the large parts of the lake regenerate during the wet season.

When Monsoon rain falls, it floods the central area between Siem Reap and Phnom Penh.

These ancient ruins of Angkor are way bigger than you would expect.

Whilst this is the biggest religious complex in the world. The grounds extend over more than 500 acres.

This equates to around 50 times larger than the site of Machu Picchu.
