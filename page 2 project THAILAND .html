THAILAND

Thailand is located in South East Asia.

It is home to cheap eats and beautiful sceneries

It wont take you long to find a party place or a fabulous beach

Here are a few pictures with a short detail of where these photos were taken and a little bit about the area

This is maya bay. This has been closed for the last two years due to covid. Recently it has reopened.

The beach is mainly famous for being the site where the Hollywood film 'the beach' was filmed.

It is incredibly picturesque with the crystral clear sea and soft white sand.

This picture was taken in Bangkok. Even though it is a large city, there are still many beauty spots in the country.

This temple in the picture is named Wat Arun, it was build in the 1800's.

It is called the "temple of dawn" and is much loved among thai buddhists.

The best time to capture a picture here is at dawn, when the sun bounces off the walls of the temple.

This picture is a tail boat. These are often used to move around the different islands in the country.

They were designed to sit low in the water, ideal for beaching on the sand.

They have been known to cope with the rough sea surprisingly well due to the high bow

This is the White Temple in chang pai, the statues and sculptures around this are actually tiny litte hands which are everywhere.

The actual name for the Temple is Wat Rong Khun, construction began in 1997.

There was a contest held to determine who would design and build the temple.
